How to Order

Follow these simple steps to get your persoonalized or premade artwork!

Step 1: Log In or Sign Up

Create an account to place an order or Log In if you already have one.

Step 2: Fill Out the Order Form

Provide details like product type, design idea, and deadline.

Step 3: Confirm Your Order

Check your email for a summary and confirm the details.

Step 4: Make a Payment

  • 50% downpayment required.
  • Payment methods: Gcash, Bank Transfer, Shopee Checkout.

Step 5: Order Processing and Delivery

Get tracking info and updates via email.

Step 6: Review and Feedback

Leave a review to help us improve!
