Vision

For Abelicitty, art is all about creating things that are visually pleasing. The process is less about rules and more about what feels right in the moment. Abelicitty draws inspiration from their idol artists, finding their unique style through the works of those they admire.The goal isn’t necessarily to convey deep messages, but simply to create art that brings joy and a sense of visual satisfaction.

Creative Beginnings

Abelicitty grew up surrounded by design, with their father being an architect, which sparked early interest in creativity. From the age of 11, she loved experimenting with stationery, creating paper designs and patterns. While many suggested following the architectural path, she found their true passion in art. What started as simple creative play has evolved into a personal and fulfilling practice that continues to grow and change with each new piece.

When the pandemic started, I ended up spending way more time on my phone than I planned. Somewhere between the endless scrolling, I stumbled across a bunch of insanely talented artists who completely changed the game for me. Their art wasn’t just cool to look at—it felt alive, full of stories and emotions that pulled me in. They became my role models, inspiring me to pick up my own tools and create. This website is all about that journey—sharing the art that inspires me and the pieces I create along the way.

These artists quickly became my role models, not just for their talent but for how real and unfiltered their work was. I found myself saving their posts, trying to break down their techniques, and just getting lost in the way they brought their ideas to life. They showed me that art doesn’t have to be perfect to be powerful—it just has to be honest. Their influence is a huge part of what got me started, and this website is my way of capturing some of that inspiration and sharing my own take on it.
