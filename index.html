Personal Favorites

Eve

A portrayal of defiance and the allure of the forbidden, Eve captures the complexity of desire and the courage to embrace the unknown. Bold contrasts and evocative details draw viewers into a moment of bold choice and self-awareness.

Afghan Refugees

This piece captures the raw emotion of seeking safety. The mother's eyes hold a mix of fear and relief, while her embrace speaks of unwavering protection. The muted tones and stark contrasts reflect the uncertainty of their journey, inviting viewers to feel the weight of their struggle and the faint glimmer of hope.

Realization

his piece embodies the powerful moment of clarity when truth becomes undeniable. With dynamic contrasts and expressive strokes, Realization illustrates the raw emotions that surface in that split second of understanding.
